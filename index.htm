Solar Servicing Financial Hardship Script for Agents

“We understand that challenges happen, and we’re here to support you. You may be eligible for our hardship program. I just ask you a few questions to understand your financial situation. Once I have the information, I’ll submit it for review. We should have an answer shortly after. This will take about 10 minutes. Is now a good time to start? If not, we can schedule a call back.”
Step 1: Verify Eligibility
  • Borrower made their first payment.
  • Loans under 90 days past due.
  • No other extension in previous 12 months (max 2 in 5 years).
  • Extension must be 1 to 3 months.
Step 2: Gather Information

Fill out the explanation fields above.

Step 3: Informing Borrower About Limits
“Please note that if approved, you may not qualify for another extension for at least 12 months. We recommend requesting a 3-month extension.”
Step 4: Submit via Slack

Paste questions/answers into #solar-serv-hardship-requests with note: “Please review hardship request for [borrower name] + [loan number].”

Step 5: Response to Borrower

If Approved: “Great news! Your request has been approved... let's set up autopay... What checking account will you be using?"

(Read mandatory interest accrual disclosure if they refuse autopay)

If Denied: “Your request has been denied due to [reason]. However, we’re still willing to work with you. Would you like to discuss payment plans?"

Step 6: Wrap-Up

Recap next due date and ensure everything is clear.

Step 7: Submitting the CRF
