AGENT COMMUNICATION SCRIPTS

1. Over-the-Phone Disclaimers

[MINI-MIRANDA]: "This is an attempt to collect a debt. Any information obtained will be used for that purpose. This call is being recorded for quality and training purposes."

2. Collection Tactics & Protocol

  • Verification: Always confirm the last 4 digits of the SSN and the current mailing address before disclosing debt details.
  • The "Silence" Technique: After presenting a settlement offer, remain silent for 10 seconds. Force the debtor to respond first.
  • Urgency Escalation: Mention the "Review Window" closing within 24 hours to encourage immediate partial payments.
  • Third-Party Disclosure: Do NOT leave debt information on voicemails or with third parties. Only leave name and callback number.
